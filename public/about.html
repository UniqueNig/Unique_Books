Our Story

For over two decades, Unique Books has been more than just a bookstore - we're a community hub where stories come to life and readers find their next adventure.

From Humble Beginnings

Unique Books began in 1998 as a small, independent bookstore in the heart of Literary City. Founded by Sarah and Michael Johnson, two literature enthusiasts with a dream to create a space where book lovers could gather, discover new authors, and share their passion for reading.

What started as a 500-square-foot shop with just 2,000 titles has grown into a beloved community institution with over 50,000 books across all genres and a vibrant online presence serving readers nationwide.

Despite our growth, we've never lost sight of our original mission: to connect readers with stories that inspire, educate, and entertain while fostering a love of literature in our community.

Community Focus

We believe bookstores should be community hubs. We host author events, book clubs, and literary discussions to bring readers together.
