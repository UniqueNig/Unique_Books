Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about our bookstore and services.

We offer several shipping options to meet your needs:

  • Standard Shipping (3-5 business days): $3.99 or FREE on orders over $25
  • Express Shipping (2 business days): $9.99
  • Overnight Shipping (1 business day): $19.99

All orders are processed within 24 hours during business days. International shipping is also available with varying rates based on destination.

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. Our return policy includes:

  • Returns are accepted within 30 days of purchase
  • Books must be in original condition with no damage
  • Original receipt or order confirmation is required
  • Refunds will be issued to the original payment method
  • Shipping costs are non-refundable unless the return is due to our error

To initiate a return, please contact our customer service team or visit our store.

  • Book Clubs: We have several genre-specific book clubs that meet monthly

  • Children's Story Time: Weekly readings for young readers

Definitely! If we don't have a book in stock, we can usually order it for you. Here's how it works:

  • Contact us with the book title, author, and format (hardcover, paperback, etc.)
  • We'll check availability with our distributors
  • Most special orders arrive within 5-10 business days
  • We'll notify you when your book arrives
  • Payment is required when the book is ordered

There's no extra fee for special orders, and you'll pay the same price as if the book was in stock. Some rare or out-of-print titles may take longer to source.

Yes, we have a used book section and we're always looking to add quality pre-owned books to our collection. Our buying process:

  • We accept books during store hours (please call ahead for large collections)
  • We evaluate books based on condition, demand, and edition
  • Payment options include store credit (higher value) or cash
  • We cannot accept books that are damaged, have odors, or are outdated textbooks

For a preliminary evaluation, you can email photos of your books to usedbooks@bookhaven.com.
